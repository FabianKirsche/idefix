OSGi tool development with oomph setups

pre-requisites

  1. installed minimal j2se-1.7
  2. download eclipseInstaller for your OS from here
  3. extract the installer and launch it
  4. now the eclipseInstaller is ready and you can continue with the proceedure below

bnd/bndtools development

  1. In the top-right menu switch to "Advanced Mode"
  2. Select "<User Products>" and press the green Plus in the top right
  3. Add User Products dialog is opened and you can copy and paste the following URL inside
    http://peterkir.github.io/idefix/oomph.osgi/product_IDEfixBndtools.setup
    and confirm with "OK".
  4. You can now select the product version you are interested in and continue with "Next >"
  5. Select "Github Projects > <User>" and press the green Plus in the top right
  6. Add User Projects dialog is opened and you can copy and paste the following URL inside
    http://peterkir.github.io/idefix/oomph.osgi/project_bndtools.setup
    and confirm with "OK"
  7. "Double-Click" the freshly appeared bndtools project (it should be selected inside the catalog at the bottom) and then press "Next"
  8. Give the defaults a try ;-) and press "Next >" and then "Finish"
  9. Wait a few minutes and work on bndtools!
