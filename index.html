OSGi tool development with oomph setups

pre-requisites

j2see-1.7 installed

Download the eclipseInstaller for your OS from here. Extract the installer and launch it. In the top-right menu switch to "Advanced Mode". Now you are ready and can continue with the proceedure below.

bnd/bndtools development

  1. Select "<User Products>" and press the green Plus in the top right
  2. Add User Products dialog is opened and you can copy and paste the following URL inside http://peterkir.github.io/idefix/oomph.osgi/product_IDEfixBndtools.setup and confirm with "OK".
  3. You can now select the product version you are interested in and continue with "Next >"
  4. Select "Github Projects > <User>" and press the green Plus in the top right
  5. Add User Projects dialog is opened and you can copy and paste the following URL inside http://peterkir.github.io/idefix/oomph.osgi/project_bndtools.setup and confirm with "OK"
  6. "Double-Click" the freshly appeared bndtools project (it should be selected inside the catalog at the bottom) and then press "Next"
  7. Give the defaults a try ;-) and press "Next >" and then "Finish"
  8. Wait a few minutes and work on bndtools!
